What makes a movie successful?

What makes a movie successful? While this question may seem like a low-hanging fruit, it requires a very holistic approach. A good film should not only make use of the 7th Art but of all seven Arts. A movie implies many choices: what genres to focus on, which director and actors bring the most added value, what budget to spend? We gather in this study movie data from multiple databases including CMU movies, IMDB and Wikipedia. With such a large movie database, we can expect to find some patterns of which feature choices lead to a more successful movie. But then again, what makes a movie successful? From profitability to perenniality, we shall investigate different success indicators and help you create the next Oscar-winning movie.

How to we quantify success?

To investigate what makes a movie successful, we need to be able to quantify success. Success of a movie can be very subjective. We thus need to diversify our definitions of success to have a holistic view of our dataset. The following success indicators are considered:

  • How well a movie is received? We base ourselves on the movie ratings from the IMDB database which we weight by the number of votes.
  • How profitable a movie is? Overall gross revenue of the movie and profit made
  • How long a movie will last in time? Number of monthly connection in 2022 to the movie wikipedia page
The top 20 movies for each of those success features are displayed below.

Taking into account those 4 success features (IMDB rating, gross revenue, profit, wikipedia pageviews), we create a single score for each movie. From there this score is used to compute for each movie feature individual i (actor, director, genre) a feature score weighted by the number of movies in which individual i plays a role.

Best actors and directors to get a good movie

Choosing a good cast and director for a movie are both crucial elements of the film-making process, as they can greatly impact the quality and success of a film.

A good cast is essential for bringing the characters to life and making them believable and relatable to the audience. The actors must be able to convey the emotions and motivations of their characters through their performances, and must be able to work well with the other members of the cast in order to create a cohesive and compelling story. In addition to acting ability, it is also important to consider the diversity and representation of the cast, as a diverse cast can bring different perspectives and experiences to the film and make it more relatable to a wider audience.

On the other hand, a good director is responsible for overseeing the creative vision of the film and bringing it to life through the actors, sets, and other elements of the film. They must be able to communicate their vision to the cast and crew and guide them towards achieving it. A good director also has the ability to bring out the best performances from the actors and create a cohesive and compelling story.

In the present study, we looked into the added value of actors and directors by assigning them a score based on their portfolio of movies (respective score and number of movies) sorted by genres. We further investigated the added values of specific collaborations when a minimum of 3 movies have been made together. On the network graphs above, we show the top 15 actors and directors according to the genre of your choice. The size of the bubble is proportional to the score of the individual while the presence and width of an edge between two individuals symbolizes the added value of their collaboration.

Overall, the cast of a movie plays a crucial role in its success, and choosing the right actors is an important decision that can have a significant impact on the film's quality and appeal.

How well this genre works with others?

We now investigate the best associations of genres. Having different genres for a movie can be important for a number of reasons.

One reason is that it allows the movie to appeal to a wider audience. Different genres appeal to different demographics, and by incorporating elements of multiple genres, a movie can attract a larger and more diverse audience.

Another reason is that it can make the movie more interesting and unpredictable. By combining different genres, a movie can keep the audience guessing and create a more unique and engaging viewing experience.

In addition, incorporating elements of different genres can also allow a movie to explore a wider range of themes and ideas. For example, a comedy-drama might explore both light-hearted and serious themes, while a horror-comedy might blend scares with laughs.

Overall, having different genres in a movie can help to make it more appealing, interesting, and dynamic, and can allow it to explore a wider range of themes and ideas.

Let's talk about money. How much you need to spend to get a successful movie?

An optimal budget is essential when making a movie for a number of reasons. First and foremost, a budget determines the scope and scale of a film. It determines how much money is available for production costs such as hiring actors, renting equipment, and paying for locations and sets. A larger budget allows for more resources to be invested in these areas, which can lead to a higher-quality film.

On the other hand, a smaller budget may limit the scope of the film and require filmmakers to be more creative and resourceful in order to achieve their vision. While it is certainly possible to make a great film on a small budget, it requires careful planning and efficient use of resources.

In addition to determining the scope and scale of a film, a budget also plays a critical role in the marketing and distribution of a movie. Marketing and distribution costs can be significant, and having an optimal budget can help ensure that a film reaches a wide audience and generates a return on investment.

Overall, an optimal budget is crucial for ensuring that a film has the resources it needs to be successful both creatively and financially. It allows filmmakers to achieve their vision and bring their stories to life, and it helps ensure that a film can reach a wide audience and generate a return on investment.
